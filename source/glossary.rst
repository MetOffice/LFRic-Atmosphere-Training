.. Glossary Conventions

   1) Keep it brief.
      If a glossary entry requires more than a few lines then it
      should probably become a page of its own.

   2) Use ".. seealso" blocks to point to  should contain, in order:
      - Other glossary items
      - internal links
      - external links

   3) Use singular rather than plural terms for glossary definitions
      (e.g. task rather than tasks).
